About Me

My name is Charlie and I am a fledgingly developer hailing from the UK but now located in beautiful Canada. I have bold aspirations to work my way down the Eastern coast of America to one day end up in the Bay area. I've been coding on and off now for about 6 months and I still have a huge amount to learn! Nonetheless, this is the first thing I have truly felt a burning drive to do and my enjoyment, and no small amount of frustration, has kept me going during both early mornings and late nights. If you're interested in learning a little bit more about me you can contact me directly or check out what I've been doing on GitHub. Thanks for the interest!
